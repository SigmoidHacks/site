# We are on a mission.

Sigmoid Hacks is a student-led hackathon powered by Hack Club and the NNSG Community. Sigmoid Hacks is a 3 day machine learning oriented hackathon that introduces students into the world of machine learning. We believe machine learning doesn't have to be just another buzzword and should instead be and we stand by this. With your help, we can inspire students to pursue their dreams and work with AI & Machine Learning.

Sigmoid Hacks is dedicated in encourage students to learn computer science—at just the age where they're interested in technology and programming.

100+ students of all skill levels will assemble for a weekend of collaboration and innovation, each building apps/websites/games that give back to the community in 12 hours. Then participants and judges will select the winning projects and teams.
